# Beans #
- 1lb Beans
- 4.5c water (chicken bone broth?)
- 1 12oz Stout beer (1.5c)
- 1tsp Liquid smoke
- 1tsp Garlic
- 4 Jalapenos
- 6-8oz Bacon (cut into strips)
- Salt and pepper to taste (Coopers)


- Add everything to pot
- manual high pressure for 40 minutes.
- Let it release pressure naturally
